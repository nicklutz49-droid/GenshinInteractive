# Writing Style Guide

## Voice & POV

- **Third person limited**, close to Aether's POV and inner thoughts.
- Aether's internal monologue is warm, slightly self-deprecating, and occasionally flustered.

## Dialogue

- Dialogue must be **distinct per character** (Alhaitham ≠ Kaveh; Hu Tao ≠ Ayaka).
- **Avoid info-dumping intros** — reveal personality through action and dialogue.

## Chapters

- Chapter titles should be **evocative and slightly literary**.
- **End each chapter on quiet emotion, anticipation, or a small meaningful beat** — not a cliffhanger.

## Romance Pacing

- **Do not rush romance.** Early chapters build friendship, familiarity, and chemistry.

## Choices

- **5–7 per chapter**, labeled.
- Meaningfully different — **not just tone variants**.
- Some choices affect which characters appear.
- Some choices have consequences that echo later.
- Dialogue choices should feel natural for Aether's awkward-but-earnest voice.
- **At least one option per set should be Aether putting his foot in his mouth.**
