# The Five Colleges

Teyvat University is organized into five major colleges, each with its own culture and campus area. Characters are associated with a home college but appear across campus.

| College | Vibe | Key Characters |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Mondstadt College of Arts & Music | Free-spirited, creative, chaotic | Venti, Diluc, Kaeya, Fischl, Eula, Jean, Amber, Lisa, Razor |
| Liyue School of Business & Culture | Disciplined, traditional, high-achieving | Keqing, Ganyu, Zhongli, Hu Tao, Xiao, Ningguang, Beidou, Xinyan, Yanfei |
| Inazuma Department of Fashion & Design | Aesthetic, intense, proud | Ayaka, Yoimiya, Kokomi, Kazuha, Sara, Thoma, Gorou, Itto, Yae Miko |
| Sumeru STEM & Research Institute | Intellectual, eccentric, passionate | Nahida, Alhaitham, Cyno, Kaveh, Tighnari, Collei, Dehya, Layla |
| Fontaine Law & Performing Arts | Dramatic, polished, competitive | Furina, Neuvillette, Navia, Clorinde, Wriothesley, Lynette, Lyney, Freminet |

## Notes

- **Chiori** runs a boutique clothing shop just off campus, familiar to students across departments.
- **Nilou** is a Fontaine performing arts major who also teaches popular campus dance fitness classes.
