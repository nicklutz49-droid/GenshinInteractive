# Story Premise

## Overview

**Teyvat University** is a slice-of-life romance interactive story set in a modern college AU of Genshin Impact.

| Aspect | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| Genre | Slice-of-life romance |
| Setting | Modern college AU of Genshin Impact |
| Tone | Warm, grounded, funny and emotional — a blend of visual novel and literary fanfic |
| Focus | Slow-burn romance + friendship & found family |
| Player character | Aether |
| Current time | Fall, Year 1 |

## Player Character

The player is **Aether**, a first-year student:

- Awkward but earnest
- Transferred from abroad
- Enrolled in the **Sumeru STEM & Research Institute**
- Lives in on-campus dorms (Sumeru wing)

## Structure & Format

- **Chapter by chapter** — one scene or day per chapter.
- **Medium chapter length** — roughly a 10–20 minute read.
- Each chapter offers **5–7 labeled choices** (A, B, C, ...).
- **Romance options are female characters only** and develop slowly.
