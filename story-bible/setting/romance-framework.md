# Romance Framework

## Rules

- Romance develops **slowly and naturally**.
- No character confesses feelings or acts overtly romantic early.
- Affection builds through repeated interactions, small moments, and meaningful choices.
- Each love interest has a distinct arc, flaws, and emotional depth.
- **Female characters only.**
- Love triangles / complicated dynamics are welcome as the story develops.
