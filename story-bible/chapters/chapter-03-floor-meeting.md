# Chapter 3 — *Floor Three, Mostly Accounted For*

> **Status:** ✅ Complete.
> **Setting:** Sumeru dorm, 3rd-floor common room (a coed floor). Fall, Year 1 — Friday, 8 PM.

---

## Summary

Aether went to Cyno's first floor meeting of the semester: a packed, too-small common
room, "load-bearing" quiet hours, an escalating run of straight-faced puns, and Collei's
homemade Mondstadt almond tofu rounds quietly stealing the evening. He met **Dehya** — a
transfer student and the floor's unofficial heart — and learned her easy confidence is
practiced generosity born from a lonely first transfer semester. Coming home, Aether
found his light on: his long-absent roommate, **Freminet**, had finally arrived (quiet,
precise, mid-repair on a dive computer). Rather than face the awkwardness alone, Aether
fetched Dehya to fold Freminet gently into the floor, getting to know her real story in
the process, and ended the night writing something true on his intro card.

---

## Choices Made

- **B)** Just opened the door on the mystery light → met Freminet directly; asked about
  the dive-computer gear instead of the missed meeting, setting an easy tone.
- **C)** Got Dehya to do the introducing → used it as a way to genuinely get to know her;
  learned her transfer backstory and the source of her generosity; folded Freminet in
  without making it a thing.
