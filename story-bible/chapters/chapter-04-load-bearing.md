> **Status:** ✅ Complete.
> **Setting:** Sumeru dorm third-floor common room → convenience store run → the low wall outside the dorm. Fall, Year 1 — a Tuesday night.

## Summary

Aether and Freminet went to Dehya's Tuesday movie night (Kaveh wept at a boat
documentary; Alhaitham's book stayed closed; Cyno landed one perfect pun). Aether spent
the night noticing that Dehya holds up the whole floor and wondering who holds her up.
Cleaning up after, he fumbled a compliment — "the mom of the floor" — that landed harder
than intended, then recovered by inviting Dehya and Collei on a midnight convenience-store
run. On the cold walk back, Dehya confided the thing she doesn't tell people: she's been
"the wall" since childhood, raising her younger brother while her mom worked nights, and
came to college hoping to put that role down — only to pick it back up here by choice.
Aether gave her the light version when she needed it, folded Collei back in, and by
midnight the three of them had become a unit: a standing Tuesday tradition, an
apology-snack precedent, and a name — **the Load-Bearing Society** (coined by Collei).
