# Chapter 2 — *A Hundred Tables and the Tyranny of Choice*

> **Status:** ✅ Complete.
> **Setting:** Autumn Activities Fair, Central Quad. Fall, Year 1 — first Saturday.

## Summary

Lumine dragged Aether to the Autumn Activities Fair, where he wandered the quad and
encountered more of the campus than he'd seen all week. He passed Hu Tao's gothic
ghost-tour table (candles, smoke machine, "body count — not metaphorically") and Venti
busking with a ukulele and improvising a song about him on the spot. On instinct he
drifted toward the STEM-adjacent row, where Collei found him and brought him to Nahida's
Akademiya Interdisciplinary Society table — cold tea, Wednesdays, bring a question.
Nahida already knew his name, room, and major. He took the flyer and moved on.

He almost walked away from the photography booth for good, then turned around. Yoimiya
photographed him mid-protest, asked him why he'd stopped at the fireworks print, got the
real answer, and gave him the photo to keep — "first-day document." The fair wound down
into a full sibling debrief with Lumine (who'd made the competitive climbing team,
naturally), a stolen granola bar, and Venti's ukulele carrying over the whole green.
