## Summary

Aether hosted his first-ever gathering — the Load-Bearing Society, just the three of
them, in room 304 while Freminet was away on a dive-club trip. Drinks (Dehya brought
"options"; "nobody's a hero tonight"), terrible snacks, Collei on aux, and a chaos dare
game Dehya steered away from "spicy" into pure dumb fun. As the night mellowed, a charged
closeness crept in. Collei — brave for once on her own behalf — named it: she has feelings
for *both* of them. Aether admitted the same. Dehya, who'd hidden behind an "I just take
care of people" wall, let herself want something back. Rather than rush it while tipsy,
Dehya set the rule that mattered: **if it's real, it's real on Tuesday, sober.** They chose
to sit with it together and be careful.
