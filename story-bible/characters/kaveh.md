## Quick Reference
> The dramatic, warm, perpetually-broke architecture student two doors down who adopts people on sight — and Alhaitham's long-suffering roommate/frenemy.

## Personality
Dramatic, artistic, big-hearted, and emotionally expressive — the tonal opposite of Alhaitham. Talks fast and with feeling, self-aware about his own theatrics ("I'm not usually like this. I am exactly like this, actually."). Generous and quick to befriend. Perpetually broke. Cares deeply about aesthetics and craft; takes art seriously and is genuinely warmed by being taken seriously in return.

## Appearance
Long blond hair. Paint-stained hands. Surrounds himself with rolled-up posters, a drafting tablet, and one very nice desk lamp.

## Background
Architecture student living in dorm room 306, one door past Alhaitham (305) — and his roommate. They've known each other for years; longtime frenemy dynamic. Kaveh insists he can't tell whether Alhaitham likes him or "merely tolerates my continued existence as a fixed cost," but acknowledges Alhaitham "notices everything."

## Relationship with Aether
- **Current status:** Stranger → **Acquaintance** → Friend → Close → Romantic Interest
- **Affection:** 3/10
- **Key moments:**
  - Pulled Aether into his room to weigh in on the "dead" north-facing light.
  - Lit up when Aether genuinely complimented his drawings; declared "We're going to be friends."
  - Extracted a promise from Aether to help with lamp placement later.

## Voice / Speech Notes
Theatrical, rapid, emotionally heightened, but warm and funny with it. Italics-worthy emphasis. Self-deprecating asides. Treats aesthetic problems as existential ones. Sample line: "Does the light in this room make you want to live, or does it make you want to lie down on the floor and slowly stop?"

## Running Threads
- Frenemy-roommate dynamic with Alhaitham (305/306).
- Ongoing "help me with my room/lamp" bit with Aether.
- Hints at being broke — money stress likely to recur.
- Recruiting Aether toward architecture / "there's still time."
