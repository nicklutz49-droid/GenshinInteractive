## Quick Reference
> The third floor's unofficial heart — a transfer student who makes sure the door never closes on the new ones.

## Personality
Direct, warm-under-gruff, physically confident and easy in space. Steps past small talk. Pays close attention to the people who need it (clocked Collei's homemade-food moment and made the room honor it). Her ease is practiced generosity, not effortless confidence.

## Appearance
Tall — properly tall, ducks under the doorframe sign. Hair in elaborate locs pulled back. Athletic build / strong presence; "comes into a room the way weather comes in."

## Background
Transferred in as a second-year from a smaller school, knew no one; landed on a floor of people already "sealed" and friend-grouped, spent a whole semester eating dinner standing up in her room. Decided no one on her watch would feel that way — hence the door-jamming. Studies materials science because she wants to do structural engineering: "build things that hold." Started Floor 3's now-permanent Tuesday movie night last spring. Lives in 308.
