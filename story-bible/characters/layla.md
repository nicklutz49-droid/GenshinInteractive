## Quick Reference
> The sweet, sleep-deprived STEM floormate who was hunting for her room (312); bonded with Aether over neither of them having it all figured out.

## Personality
Anxious, gentle, and self-deprecating, running on very little sleep. Apologizes reflexively ("I keep sitting in places"). Wry and quietly funny once she relaxes. Visibly relieved by small kindnesses; touched to meet someone who admits they don't have everything figured out.

## Appearance
Dark hair, soft shadows under her eyes — the look of someone who "hasn't slept since the entrance exams." Carrying a single unopened box.

## Background
A STEM student on Aether's floor, assigned to room 312 (odd/even numbers split at the stairwell). On move-in day she couldn't find it and was half-convinced the building was rearranging itself on purpose.
