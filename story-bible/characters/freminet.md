## Quick Reference
> Aether's long-absent roommate — a quiet, precise diver who fixes his own gear and braces for judgment that rarely comes.

## Personality
Quiet, orderly, precise. Apologizes reflexively. Freezes rather than panics. Honest once he stops apologizing. Handles things he loves with great care. Warms slowly and genuinely.

## Appearance
About Aether's age, slight, dark hair falling across his forehead. Stillness that reads as bracing.

## Background
Mechanical engineering major from Fontaine. Arrived late to the dorm — got the meeting days wrong and then didn't come once he realized ("I've been told it's a pattern"). Made his bed with unusual precision, keeps a backpack at a right angle, brought a small succulent in a white pot. Dives; was recalibrating a dive computer's gear train by hand with jeweler's tweezers when Aether walked in (shops "strip the threads").

## Relationship with Aether
- **Current status:** Stranger → **Acquaintance** → Friend → Close → Romantic Interest
- **Affection:** 3/10
- **Key moments:**
  - Caught off guard when Aether opened the door.
  - Aether asked about the gear instead of the missed meeting, which let him relax.
  - Thanked Aether "for not making it a thing."
  - Agreed (reluctantly, then sincerely) that Dehya is "a lot — in the best way."

## Voice / Speech Notes
Soft, careful, apologetic, precise. Opens up when the topic is something he loves (diving, mechanisms). Sample: "It's easier if you do it yourself. Most shops strip the thread when they service the casing."
