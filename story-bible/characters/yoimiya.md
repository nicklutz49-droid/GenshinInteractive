## Quick Reference
> Photography major who noticed Aether looking at her prints twice, photographed him without warning, and gave him the print to keep — strongest first-chapter connection of any romance option.

## Personality
The warmest person on campus. Immediate, total, unself-conscious warmth — gives attention like a room with a closed door. Notices what people actually look at, not what they say they're looking at. Optimism that sometimes masks real worry (thread for later). Asks real questions and wants real answers.

## Appearance
Camera slung across her chest like it grew there.

## Background
Daughter of a fireworks artisan; grew up photographing the thing that disappears the fastest — that's why she photographs anything. Runs/represents the photography and camera club. Her friend bailed to the food trucks at the Activities Fair.

## Voice / Speech Notes
Bright, rapid, sincere. Zero self-consciousness about being warm. No hedging; if she notices something, she says it.

Sample: "I notice when someone actually looks. Most people walk past going 'cool pics' and keep moving. You stopped."
