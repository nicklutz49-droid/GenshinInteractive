# Third-Floor Common Room

| Field | Value |
|---|---|

## Description
The shared common room for Floor 3 of the Sumeru dorm. Designed for a smaller population than it now serves — chairs dragged in from the hallway, a floor cushion or two, standing room on meeting nights. The social heart of the floor.

## Notable Features
- Whiteboard (Cyno's meeting HQ; "FOUNDATIONS OF COEXISTENCE")
- The intro / social corkboard ("FLOOR 3: WE LIVE HERE AND WE MEAN IT") — event cards, study-group sign-ups, an introductions + question honor system
- Kitchen sign-up sheet, laundry schedule, bathroom allocation diagrams
- Hosts the floor's Tuesday movie night (Dehya's institution)

## Characters Associated
- Cyno (RA — runs meetings here)
- Dehya, Collei, Layla, Kaveh, Alhaitham, Aether, Freminet (Floor 3 residents)
