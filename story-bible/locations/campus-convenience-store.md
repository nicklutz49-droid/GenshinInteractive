# Campus Convenience Store

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Area / College** | Off the dorm quad, walkable from the Sumeru wing |
| **First visited** | Chapter 4 — Load-Bearing |

## Description
The late-night convenience store on the far corner from the dorms — open late, glowing
"like a lighthouse for the under-fed." Bored cashier, humming coolers, too-bright lights.
The natural terminus of a midnight run.

## Notable Features
- Open late (the go-to for after-movie-night food)
- Instant noodles, ice cream (walk-home melt risk), single midnight bananas of dubious moral status
- A vending machine elsewhere on campus once ate Collei's money (ongoing grudge)

## Characters Associated
- The Load-Bearing Society (Aether, Dehya, Collei) — their post-movie-night ritual destination

## Scenes That Happened Here
- **Ch 4:** Midnight run after movie night; on the walk back, Dehya confided her history; the trio friend group formed on the low wall outside the dorm afterward
