# Sumeru Dormitory

| Field | Value |
|---|---|

## Description

The residence hall for Sumeru's STEM students. It was apparently designed by "someone who loved plants more than students" — vines creep up the walls of the inner courtyard, and the hallways carry a permanent smell of green tea and old paper. Little potted plants (ferns, a notably sad cactus, and assorted unnamed greenery) sit on the windowsills like tiny guardians. On move-in day it's loud and chaotic, packed with arriving students and their families.

## Notable Features

- Plant-obsessed architecture: courtyard vines, windowsill ferns, one sad cactus.
- Hallways smell of green tea and old paper.
- Third floor houses Aether and several other STEM students.
- Friday floor meetings, held with snacks.
- The radiators reportedly make a sound "like a dying whale" around 2am — structural, per Alhaitham.
- Multi-floor building: Lumine lives three floors up, on the 6th floor.

## Characters Associated

- **Aether** — resident, third floor (Room 304).
- **Cyno** — third-floor RA.
- **Alhaitham** — resident, Room 305 (shares a wall with Aether).
- **Lumine** — resident, 6th floor.
