# Central Quad

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Area / College** | University-wide / shared |
| **First visited** | Chapter 2 — A Hundred Tables and the Tyranny of Choice |

## Description
The large central green shared by all five colleges — the one place on campus where
every college overlaps on the same physical space. Flanked by college buildings on
multiple sides. In good weather it's a crossroads; on event days it becomes a kingdom.

## Notable Features
- Tables stretched in long ragged rows across the whole green on fair day
- Each college bleeds a different color into the banners and décor
- Smoke machine (source: Hu Tao's booth, confirmed)
- Clothesline of photography prints strung between poles (Yoimiya's booth)
- Fountain at one end — dance demonstrations cluster here
- Food trucks accessible nearby
- Venti's ukulele audible from anywhere in the quad

## Characters Associated
- **Hu Tao** — ghost tour table (black draping, real candles)
- **Venti** — music/busking table; sometimes just busks here unprompted
- **Nahida** — Akademiya Society table (hand-lettered cloth sign, pot of tea)
- **Yoimiya** — photography/camera club table (clothesline of prints)
- **Collei** — found wandering the fair
