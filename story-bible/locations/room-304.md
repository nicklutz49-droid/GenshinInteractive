## Description

Aether's dorm room on the third floor of the Sumeru wing. Small, but "honest about it": two beds, two desks, and one window overlooking the inner courtyard. The walls are institutional beige, and the room still smells of cardboard and fresh paint. As of Chapter 1, Aether's roommate had not yet arrived — the door was still locked and the name tag present but unfilled.
