# Room 304

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Area / College** | Sumeru STEM & Research Institute (Sumeru wing, third floor) |
| **First visited** | Chapter 1 — Move-In Day |

## Description

Aether's dorm room on the third floor of the Sumeru wing. Small, but "honest about it": two beds, two desks, and one window overlooking the inner courtyard. The walls are institutional beige, and the room still smells of cardboard and fresh paint. As of Chapter 3, the long-empty second side is finally claimed: **Freminet** — a mechanical engineering major from Fontaine — has arrived as Aether's roommate.

## Notable Features

- Two beds, two desks, one window facing the inner courtyard.
- Institutional beige walls; smells of cardboard and fresh paint.
- Name tags hand-lettered in green marker, with a little doodled star next to "AETHER."
- Shares a wall with Room 305 (Alhaitham).
- As of Ch 3, Freminet's side is set up: a meticulously made bed, a backpack kept at a right angle, and a small succulent in a white pot on the windowsill.
- Freminet works on dive equipment here (recalibrating a dive computer's gear train by hand with jeweler's tweezers).

## Characters Associated

- **Aether** — occupant.
- **Freminet** — Aether's roommate (mechanical engineering, Fontaine); arrived as of Ch 3.
- **Alhaitham** — next door in Room 305 (shared wall).

## Scenes That Happened Here
- **Ch 1:** Aether arrives and settles into the half-empty room; notices the doodled star on the green-marker name tag and the still-locked, unclaimed roommate side.
- **Ch 3:** Freminet revealed as the second occupant; first roommate meeting over a disassembled dive computer.
