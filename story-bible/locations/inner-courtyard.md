## Description

The central courtyard of the Sumeru dorm, the heart of the building's plant-loving design. Vines climb the surrounding walls, and golden afternoon light slants across the greenery. It's visible directly from Room 304's window. As move-in afternoon wore on, the crowds gradually thinned out and the courtyard grew quieter.
