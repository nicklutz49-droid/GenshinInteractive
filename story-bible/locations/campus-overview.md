# Campus Overview

| Field | Value |

## Description

Teyvat University is a large, prestigious institution made up of five major colleges, each with its own distinct culture and campus area. The grounds are maze-like and easy to get lost in — Aether got lost twice in the first week, and Collei managed three times. This document is a lightweight reference; most areas are referenced but not yet visited in-story.

## Notable Features

**The Five Colleges:**
- **Mondstadt College of Arts & Music** — free-spirited, creative, chaotic.
- **Liyue School of Business & Culture** — disciplined, traditional, high-achieving.
- **Inazuma Department of Fashion & Design** — aesthetic, intense, proud.
- **Sumeru STEM & Research Institute** — intellectual, eccentric, passionate (Aether's college).
- **Fontaine Law & Performing Arts** — dramatic, polished, competitive.

**Key off-campus / shared spots:**
- **The Library** — a busker (Venti) plays a tiny guitar outside it.
- **The Dining Hall** — chaotic, located near the library.
- **Chiori's Boutique** — a clothing shop just off campus; a familiar student hangout.
- **Dance Fitness Classes** — taught by Nilou; popular across campus.
- **The Campus Garden** — where Kazuha is often found.

## Characters Associated

- **Aether** — student at the Sumeru STEM & Research Institute.
- **Venti** — busker outside the library.
- **Chiori** — runs the off-campus boutique.
- **Nilou** — teaches the popular dance fitness classes.
- **Kazuha** — frequently found in the campus garden.
- **Collei** — fellow student; got lost three times her first week.

## Scenes That Happened Here
- **Ch 1:** Only the Sumeru wing has been visited so far; the wider campus and its other four colleges are referenced but not yet explored.
